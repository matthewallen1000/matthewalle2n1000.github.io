Frequently asked questions

  • Is the first class free?
    YES! The first class is free. You should never pay to study with someone without being given the opportunity to sit in and see him teach.
  • How to register?
    Sign up here on this page! You will receive a confirmation email that will include the link to the zoom class as well as our suggestions for to experience this class the best (IE: Headphones are a big plus)
  • How long will the class be?
    It is scheduled for 60 minutes, but I will stay on after the class to answer everyone's questions no matter how long that takes.
  • What do I need to prepare?
    Nothing! We recommend you have some way of taking notes though.
  • Can I bring jokes that I have already written?
    Yes! If you have written jokes please bring them with you - also our TA Frank will check in with everyone prior to the class - let him know if you have jokes prepared
  • Will you show me how to write jokes?
    Yes! And more than that we are going to show you how to format your jokes so they don't sound like you are reading an essay in front of the class in school. Also we are going to show you how to build a daily routine of writing that will leave you buried in material. You are going to love it!
  • How old do I need to be?
    The youngest professional comedian I worked with was Billy Callahan in NYC - he was 13. So that's my rule. Anyone 13 and over is welcome
  • I don't like dirty jokes - Isn't stand up comedy only dirty jokes?
    No! I love clean comedy! I also enjoy dirty humor, but there understand that there is a time and a place for it. The techniques I teach work well for clean and for "blue" comedians
  • I tell offensive jokes that only my friends laugh at, can you help me?
    YES! I used to be you when I started! Your friends share an emotional short hand with you that others do not - I'll show you how to translate it to the general public so they get you the same way your friends get you.
  • I liked the free class, is there any way to pay to continue?
    After the the free class is over we will provide everyone who attended the first free class with a link to the paid class that they can use if they wish to continue.
