I started comedy 18 years ago in NYC at the legendary Boston Comedy Club opening for David Chappelle, Bill Burr, and the likes.

I literally bombed over 1,000+ shows and honed my craft through hard work and rigorous analysis of today’s best comedians.

For the first time ever, I’m passing my unparalleled comedy experience from years of trial and error to you — absolutely free.

Does taking my class guarantee you’ll become a comedian great? No, of course not.

What I can guarantee is that once you learn the universal rules from my Laugh Class,
